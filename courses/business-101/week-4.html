Week 4: Marketing Principles

Week: 4
Topic: Marketing Principles
Duration: 3 hours

Learning Objectives

  • Define marketing and understand its importance in business
  • Understand the marketing mix (4 Ps)
  • Identify target markets and customer segmentation
  • Recognize different marketing strategies
  • Apply marketing concepts to product development

Topics Covered

  • What is marketing? Core concepts and definitions
  • The Marketing Mix: Product, Price, Place, Promotion
  • Market segmentation and target marketing
  • Consumer behavior fundamentals
  • Digital marketing vs. traditional marketing
  • Brand building and positioning

Activities

  • Current Events: Recent marketing campaigns discussion (20 min)
  • Lecture: Marketing principles and the 4 Ps (70 min)
  • Group Project: Develop a marketing plan for a new product (60 min)
  • Presentations: Group marketing plan pitches (40 min)
  • Peer Feedback: (10 min)

Readings

  • Chapter 4: Marketing Principles
  • Article: "The Evolution of Digital Marketing"
  • Case Study: "Coca-Cola's Global Marketing Strategy"
  • Supplemental: "Understanding Consumer Psychology"

Assignments

  • Read Chapter 5 for next week
  • Quiz 2: Chapters 3-4 (due before next class)
  • Individual assignment: Create a marketing strategy for your business plan project
  • Analyze a successful brand's marketing approach (1-2 pages)
