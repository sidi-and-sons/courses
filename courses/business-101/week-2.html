Week 2: Forms of Business Ownership

Week: 2
Topic: Forms of Business Ownership
Duration: 3 hours

Learning Objectives

  • Distinguish between different forms of business ownership
  • Understand the advantages and disadvantages of each ownership type
  • Identify legal and tax implications for different business structures
  • Analyze which business structure is appropriate for different scenarios

Topics Covered

  • Sole Proprietorship: characteristics and considerations
  • Partnership: general and limited partnerships
  • Corporation: C-Corp and S-Corp structures
  • Limited Liability Company (LLC)
  • Franchising as a business model
  • Cooperatives and other ownership forms

Activities

  • Review: Week 1 key concepts (15 min)
  • Lecture: Business ownership structures (70 min)
  • Case Study: Analyzing business structure decisions (40 min)
  • Group Work: Compare and contrast ownership types (45 min)
  • Class Discussion: Sharing group findings (30 min)

Readings

  • Chapter 2: Forms of Business Ownership
  • Case Study: "Choosing the Right Business Structure"
  • Legal brief: "Understanding LLC vs. Corporation"

Assignments

  • Read Chapter 3 for next week
  • Complete worksheet: "Comparing Business Ownership Forms"
  • Quiz 1: Chapters 1-2 (due before next class)
