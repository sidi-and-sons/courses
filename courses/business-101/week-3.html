Week 3: Management and Leadership

Week: 3
Topic: Management and Leadership
Duration: 3 hours

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the four functions of management
  • Distinguish between management and leadership
  • Identify different management styles and their effectiveness
  • Recognize the importance of organizational structure
  • Apply management concepts to real-world scenarios

Topics Covered

  • The four functions: Planning, Organizing, Leading, Controlling
  • Management levels: Top, middle, and first-line managers
  • Leadership styles: autocratic, democratic, laissez-faire
  • Management skills: technical, human relations, conceptual
  • Organizational charts and structures
  • Decision-making processes

Activities

  • Quiz Review: Discuss Quiz 1 results (20 min)
  • Lecture: Management functions and leadership (65 min)
  • Video: "Great Leaders in Business" documentary excerpt (25 min)
  • Role Play: Management scenarios (50 min)
  • Reflection: Personal leadership style assessment (20 min)

Readings

  • Chapter 3: Management and Leadership
  • Article: "The Difference Between Managers and Leaders"
  • Case Study: "Transformational Leadership at Apple"

Assignments

  • Read Chapter 4 for next week
  • Complete leadership style self-assessment
  • Write a two-page analysis of a successful manager/leader
  • Submit draft outline of business plan project
