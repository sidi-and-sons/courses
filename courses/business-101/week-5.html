Week 5: Financial Management Basics

Week: 5
Topic: Financial Management Basics
Duration: 3 hours

Learning Objectives

  • Understand basic financial statements and their purpose
  • Read and interpret income statements and balance sheets
  • Recognize the importance of cash flow management
  • Calculate basic financial ratios
  • Apply financial concepts to business decision-making

Topics Covered

  • Introduction to accounting and finance
  • The three main financial statements: Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow Statement
  • Assets, liabilities, and equity
  • Revenue, expenses, and profit
  • Basic financial ratios: profitability, liquidity, efficiency
  • Budgeting fundamentals

Activities

  • Quiz Review: Discuss Quiz 2 results (20 min)
  • Lecture: Introduction to financial management (60 min)
  • Workshop: Reading financial statements (45 min)
  • Practice Problems: Calculate financial ratios (40 min)
  • Case Analysis: Evaluating a company's financial health (35 min)

Readings

  • Chapter 5: Financial Management
  • Article: "Understanding Financial Statements for Non-Accountants"
  • Case Study: "Financial Analysis of a Small Business"
  • Supplemental: "The Importance of Cash Flow Management"

Assignments

  • Complete financial statement analysis worksheet
  • Develop a basic budget for your business plan project
  • Begin preparing for midterm exam (Chapters 1-5)
