Week 1: Introduction to Business and Economic Systems

Week: 1
Topic: Introduction to Business and Economic Systems
Duration: 3 hours

Learning Objectives

  • Define business and understand its role in society
  • Identify the main economic systems and their characteristics
  • Understand supply and demand fundamentals
  • Recognize the difference between profit and non-profit organizations

Topics Covered

  • What is Business? Defining core concepts
  • The role of business in the economy
  • Economic systems: capitalism, socialism, mixed economies
  • Supply and demand basics
  • Introduction to profit vs. non-profit organizations

Activities

  • Ice Breaker: Student introductions and career goals (15 min)
  • Lecture: Introduction to business concepts (60 min)
  • Discussion: Economic systems comparison (30 min)
  • Group Exercise: Identify local businesses and categorize by type (45 min)
  • Q&A Session: (30 min)

Readings

  • Chapter 1: The World of Business
  • Supplemental: "Understanding Economic Systems" article

Assignments

  • Read Chapter 2 for next week
  • Write a one-page reflection on why you're interested in studying business
  • Research and identify three local businesses of different types
